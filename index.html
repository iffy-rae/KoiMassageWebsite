Massage Services

Swedish:

The most popular massage, Swedish massage targets the top layers of muscle to provide quick relief to sore and tense muscles. This massage is best for those looking for a more gentle and relaxing experience.

Deep Tissue:

Deep tissue massage helps relieve physical and psychological pain by targeting deep layers of muscle and surrounding areas. By relieving knots, stress and injuries deep tissue can help provide every day relief. Unlike Swedish style, deep tissue can often prove more painful for patients, and so this massage is best for those wanting to alleviate deep pains.

Lymphatic Drainage:

Lymphatic Drainage is a gentle form of massage designed to to relieve swelling caused by the lymphatic system as well as relieve allergy and autoimmune symptoms. This is done by stimulating lymph fluid movement. Lymphatic drainage is also a great way to remove waste and toxins from one's body.

Prenatal Massage:

Prenatal Massage is customized for pregnant women. It is a massage aimed to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation and mobility, and soothe other pregnancy related pains.
